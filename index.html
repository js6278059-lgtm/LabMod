How to Play

1. Scientific Simulation (Sandbox Mode)

LabMod is a hard science sandbox. Elements have real physical properties.

  • Thermodynamics: Heat transfers between elements based on Specific Heat and Thermal Conductivity. Phases change at exact Melting/Boiling points (Kelvin).
  • Chemistry: Reactions occur based on proximity, activation energy, and concentration. Use the detailed reaction editor to define catalysts, byproducts, and yields.
  • Pressure: Gases build up pressure in enclosed spaces. High pressure can cause state changes or explosions.
  • Controls: Press Space to pause/play, C to clear the grid, Ctrl+Z to undo, Ctrl+Y to redo, and T to toggle Tap Mode.

2. Join Mode (Platforming)

When you click Join on a sandbox, you spawn as a circle inside the physics simulation!

  • Movement: Use A and D to move left and right.
  • Jumping: Use W or Space to jump.
  • Death: If you touch dangerous elements (Toxicity > 0, Lava, Acid), you will die and respawn.
  • Stats: Use the Up/Down/Left/Right Arrow Keys to adjust your Jump Height and Move Speed.

3. Mods & Elements (Scientific Modding)

Create custom elements with real scientific accuracy in the Mods menu.

  • Thermodynamic Properties: Set Melting Point (K), Boiling Point (K), Specific Heat (J/g·K), and Thermal Conductivity.
  • Chemical Properties: Define Toxicity, Corrosivity, pH Level, and Electrical Conductivity.
  • Advanced Reactions: Set Activation Energy, Catalysts, Byproducts, and Reaction Rates.
  • Avatar Editor: Go to your Profile and click the pencil icon on your avatar to open the Pixel Texture Customizer!
  • Visual Scripting: Click "Open Visual Script" in the Mod Creator to code element behaviors using drag-and-drop blocks!
  • Frame Animation: Click "Edit Frames" in the Mod Creator to create animated elements with multiple textures!

4. Saves System

Use the Saves button on the main menu to manage your game saves.

  • Create Save: Captures your entire game state including mods, sandboxes, account, and settings.
  • Load Save: Restores the game to the exact state when the save was created.
  • Download/Import: Export saves as .labsave.json files to backup or share with friends.
  • Customize: Rename and manage your saves just like sandboxes and mods.

5. Settings & Visuals

Customize your experience in the Settings menu.

  • Theme Mode: Switch between Dark Mode and Light Mode. The entire UI adapts instantly.
  • Simulation: Toggle Temperature and Pressure simulations independently for performance or pure visual focus.
  • Global Texture Res: Change the resolution of ALL default textures in real-time.
